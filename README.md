This project focuses on conducting a comprehensive analysis of sales, profit, and customer behavior data. The primary goal is to uncover valuable insights and provide data-driven recommendations to improve business efficiency.

The analysis covers various aspects, including:

Sales and Profit Trends: Studying seasonal fluctuations and overall growth patterns.

Product Category Analysis: Identifying profitability and margin variations across categories.

Regional Performance: Examining profit and discount impacts by region and state.

Customer Segmentation: Grouping customers using RFM analysis to develop tailored strategies.

Shipping Methods: Evaluating profitability and efficiency of different delivery options.

This is a learning project completed as part of skill-building in Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and data visualization using R, carried out during studies at IronHack.
